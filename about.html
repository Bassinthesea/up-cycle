About Up-Cycle

How It Started

It started off a project but now it's bigger than that.

We joined a program called IFE(Institute For Entrepreneurship) in hopes to learn more

about the business side of society. As a result, we were tasked to create a company

based off of our beliefs and what we wanted bring more attention to. Through our

multiple brainstorms and mock businesses we broke it down to dedicating a business

towards the beautification and cleanliness of our community.

Mission

Our mission at Up-Cycle is to primarily clean up NYC in a

way that allows for the community to benefit from it. We

wish to do so through workshops and the production and

distribution of eco-friendly clothing. While also hosting

community voluntary cleanups.

Vision

The initial impact we wish our company

would bring would be the clean up of streets in

under-represented communties. As for what

we have our sights set on the distant future, we hope

to start and develop workshops that inspire

more people to care about the world we live on.

Partnership Plans

Everywhere

Everywhere is a clothing company that recycles waste from textile industries into clothes. We want to partner with Everywhere in hopes that they become our wholesaler for their eco-friendly clothes.

EcoEnclose

EcoEnclose is an eco-friendly packaging system that we hope to partner with to bring eco-friendliness into every aspect of our company, even delivery!
